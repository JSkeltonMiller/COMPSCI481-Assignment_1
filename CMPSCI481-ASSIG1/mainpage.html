This is Milwaukee

Welcome to Cream City, the home of Miller, the place where new ties are formed, people grow, and leave. We are very happy to have you here.


This webpage will link you to the wondrous events scattered around Milwaukee. There's an event for most, with gaming societies, sporting goods stores, and books galore!


The refined gamer might appreciate goods from Boardgame Barrister, while the excitable may prefer a visit to the eSports Community. The Book lovers will find clubs


of many flavors scattered around Milwaukee, while those looking to expand their collection may appreciate some of the choice stores and lending outlets (libraries).


Many will appreciate the outdoor adventures, be it Rock Climbing, working out, or simply stocking up on exercise goods. As long as you're not a violent criminal, you're welcome here.
